Risk Flag Reference
POSTURE — awkward angles, bent back, twisted spine, neck strain
FORCE — high grip, push/pull loads, impact tools
REPETITION — same motion >4x/min, pinch grips, static holds
REACH — extended beyond elbow, above shoulder, below knee
VIBRATION — tool or whole-body vibration
SURFACE — concrete, unstable, ladder/elevated work
BLIND OPS — operator cannot see the work point
